Investigative Reporter

David Migoya

  • @davidmigoya
  • dmigoya@denverpost.com

David writes investigative projects and has been at The Denver Post since 1999. He was a founding member of the investigations team before moving on to write about banking, finance, human services and consumer affairs, then returned to investigations. David has also worked at publications in New York City, St. Louis and Detroit over a 36-year career that began at the Post in 1983.

His work has been recognized by Investigative Reporters & Editors, the Society of Professional Journalists, the Scripps Howard Foundation, the Society of Business Editors and Writers, and the National Association of Real Estate Editors. His 2018 expose about hidden court cases across Colorado was chosen as the states best by the Colorado Press Association.

Location: Denver, Colorado

Languages: English, Spanish

Awards: Best of Show -- Editorial, Best Investigative Story Package, Public Service Award, and Best Series for "Shrouded Justice" from the Colorado Press Association, 2018

Expertise: Investigative journalism

Expertise: Banking
